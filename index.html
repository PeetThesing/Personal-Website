How does it work?

Two Teams, 2 Halftimes with 30 Minutes with Jams max. 2 Minutes. Each Jam 4 Blockers and 1 Jammer compete against each other.

Nerd Alert

There are a lot of IT-People in Roller Derby, who program a Scoreboard and make it easier to officiate.
